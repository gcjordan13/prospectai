# **App Name**: Prospector AI

## Core Features:

- Prospecting Goal Input: Users can input their prospecting goals for their specific company through a text-based conversation UI.
- AI Scope Confirmation Tool: The AI will engage in a conversation to confirm the scope of the user's prospecting request, clarifying industry, location, and company size.
- Company/Website Identification: The AI tool will identify relevant companies and websites based on the confirmed prospecting goals.
- Ethical Data Scraping: The tool ethically scrapes publicly available contact information from identified companies and websites.
- Contact Information Display: Presents the scraped contact information to the user in a structured and easily accessible format, respecting data privacy standards.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark forest green (#386641) to convey trustworthiness and growth.
- Background color: Very light muted green (#F2F4F3). Visibly of the same hue as the primary color, but heavily desaturated and bright.
- Accent color: Muted blue (#467380), analogous to green, to signal stability and reliability.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font for a clean, modern and objective look.
- Simple, professional icons to represent data, contacts, and prospecting tools.
- Clean, organized layout with clear visual hierarchy to easily navigate and interpret prospecting data.
- Subtle loading animations and transitions to provide a smooth user experience during data retrieval.
